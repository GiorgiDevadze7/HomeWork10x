History of Georgia

Georgia, located at the crossroads of Europe and Asia, has a rich cultural and historical heritage. It is one of the oldest countries in the world, with a history dating back to the 6th century BC. Throughout history, Georgia has been a significant hub for trade, culture, and arts, influenced by many civilizations including Greek, Roman, and Persian. It is well known for its ancient winemaking traditions and stunning landscapes.
